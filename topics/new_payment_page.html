Making New Payment task’s UI page: UIFT03.01.01

New Payment page is a task UI page that specially designed to help clients follow all needed steps to make new payment from one of client's accounts to а payee.

Depending on what the payment is, domestic or foreign, corresponding UI page is opening. You may switching Domestic Payment and SEPA Payment pages by selecting To Czech Republic or To foreign country. UI controls that represent task Steps within the Domestic Payment and SEPA Payment pages may differ.

Whether the destination is domestic or international: this intelligent form adapts itself to the data you enter, e.g., if you select a German contact or simply start with an German IBAN, I will automatically switch to the SEPA transfer form.

Domestic payment Transfers to another bank account within your own country have 3 mandatory fields only: Name, Account number and Amount. All other fields are optional.
